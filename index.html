Dr. Banani Adhikari (Das)

I am a retired physics professor with extensive experience in the field of liquid crystals. My expertise includes electro-optical studies, research and development of materials for liquid crystal displays, X-ray diffraction studies, nuclear magnetic resonance measurements, and physical characterization of liquid crystalline materials. With a distinguished career marked by numerous awards and accolades, I have made significant contributions to the field of liquid crystals.

Explore my publications, projects, books, awards, and expertise to learn more about my work and achievements.
